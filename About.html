Bio Bio

I've been making games professionally since 2022. I joined ARVORE in 2021 as IT Tech Support and made the transition into Game Design from inside the studio, shipping multiplayer titles for Meta Quest: Blumhouse Horrorverse (as Lead Game/Level Designer), The Office World, Universal Monsters Arena, YUKI MRcade, Pixel Ripped 1995: On the Road and Coffee Fest (as Lead Game Designer). The work spans initial concepts, level design, balancing, documentation (GDD, LDD) and Live Ops. Faço jogos profissionalmente desde 2022. Entrei na ARVORE em 2021 como IT Tech Support e fiz a transição para o Game Design de dentro do estúdio, lançando títulos multiplayer para Meta Quest: Blumhouse Horrorverse (como Lead Game/Level Designer), The Office World, Universal Monsters Arena, YUKI MRcade, Pixel Ripped 1995: On the Road e Coffee Fest (como Lead Game Designer). O trabalho cobre conceitos iniciais, level design, balanceamento, documentação (GDD, LDD) e Live Ops.

I hold a Tech Degree in Digital Games from FATEC Americana. During college, I researched Serious Games as a Scientific Initiation student and founded GAMINP, a study and incentive group for game development. Sou formado em Tecnologia em Jogos Digitais pela FATEC Americana. Durante a faculdade, pesquisei Serious Games como aluno de Iniciação Científica e fundei o GAMINP, um grupo de estudo e incentivo ao desenvolvimento de jogos.

I love game jams (most of my personal projects were born in one) and I'm always learning: game and level design, UI tech art, production and beyond. I'm based in São Paulo, Brazil and work in English and Portuguese. Off the clock, you'll usually find me playing games, going out to eat with friends, reading or watching series.
